Nodes

These are the individual steps in your automation. A node can be an app (like Gemini or Gmail), a database, or a logic function (like "If...then").

Workflows

A workflow is a series of connected nodes on the n8n canvas. It's the complete visual map of your entire automated process from start to finish.

Connections

These are the lines that link your nodes, allowing data to flow from one step to the next. You control exactly what data moves where.

Triggers

This is the special node that starts your workflow. It can be a schedule (e.g., "every Monday at 9 AM"), a new email, or a webhook from another app.
